Visit Sarıyer

Discover the natural beauty and cultural heritage in this
vibrant coastal district of Istanbul.

Top Four Activities to do at Sarıyer

Emirgan Park

You can have a picnic in Emirgan Park without leaving the city and enjoy a peaceful nature getaway.

Atatürk Urban Forest

Take a nature walk, go cycling, or simply enjoy the fresh air at Atatürk Urban Forest.

Büyükdere Library

Spend some quiet time reading or studying at Büyükdere Library in Sarıyer.

Bosphorus

Stroll along the Bosphorus coast and enjoy the stunning sea views.

Where to eat what in Sarıyer?

Sarıyer Börek at Tarihi
Sarıyer Börekçisi

The famous Sarıyer Börek from Tarihi Sarıyer Börekçisi offers a crispy and delicious experience. An ideal place to savor traditional flavors.

Fresh Fish at Yeniköy
Sandal Balık

Savor a selection of fresh fish at Yeniköy Sandal Balık. A perfect place to enjoy the best seafood in Istanbul.

Coffee and Desserts at Büyükdere İskelesi

Relax with a coffee and sweet treats at Büyükdere İskelesi. Enjoy a delightful view along with your refreshments.

Breakfast at Emirgan Sütiş

Enjoy a delightful Turkish breakfast at Emirgan Sütiş while taking in the beautiful sea view.
A perfect start to your day.
